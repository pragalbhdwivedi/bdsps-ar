Scan the printed banner

This prototype uses MindAR image tracking. Point your camera at the banner artwork used to generate targets.mind.

When the banner is detected, a floating school card and logo panel will lock onto it, plus two action buttons.

  • Use the exact same banner artwork you compiled as the target.
  • Print quality matters. Blurry vinyl equals sad computer vision.
  • Use HTTPS hosting. Camera permissions get grumpy otherwise.
